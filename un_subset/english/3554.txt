UNITED NATIONS Distr. GENERAL A/C.5/52/1/Add.3 23 March 1998 ORIGINAL: ENGLISH Fifty-second session FIFTH COMMITTEE ALLOCATION OF AGENDA ITEMS TO THE FIFTH COMMITTEE Addendum Letter dated 23 March 1998 from the Acting President of the General Assembly to the Chairman of the Fifth Committee I have the honour to inform you that, at the 81st plenary meeting of the fifty-second session of the General Assembly, held on Monday, 23 March 1998, the General Assembly decided to reopen and again allocate to the Fifth Committee, under agenda item 17 (Appointments to fill vacancies in subsidiary organs and other appointments), the following sub-item: "(e) Appointment of members of the United Nations Administrative Tribunal". (Signed) Hasan ABU-NIMAH
