Peacebuilding Commission Fourth session Letter dated 16 December 2009 from the Chairperson of the Peacebuilding Commission addressed to the Permanent Representative of Comoros to the United Nations I have the honour to refer to your letter dated 9 December 2009 expressing the interest of the Comoros in participating in the work of the Peacebuilding Commission's Burundi configuration. I am pleased to inform you that the request contained in your letter has met with no objection from the members of the Organizational Committee. Accordingly, the Comoros is invited to participate in all future meetings of the Burundi configuration, in accordance with paragraph 7 (b) of General Assembly resolution 60/180 and Security Council resolution 1645 (2005).
