Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) Note verbale dated 1 March 2012 from the Permanent Mission of Chile to the United Nations addressed to the President of the Security Council The Permanent Mission of Chile to the United Nations presents its compliments to the President of the Security Council, and would like to inform him that on 21 February 2012, Decree No. 127, of 27 September 2011, was published in the Official Gazette of the Republic of Chile, implementing Security Council resolutions 1874 (2009) and 1928 (2010) and introducing measures against the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, a copy of which is attached herewith.* It should also be noted that, by taking this step, Chile has incorporated these measures into its domestic legal system, implementing and giving effect to the aforementioned resolutions of the Security Council.
