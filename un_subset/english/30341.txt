Fifty-ninth session Agenda item 113 Scale of assessments for the apportionment of the expenses of the United Nations Letter dated 7 March 2005 from the Secretary-General to the President of the General Assembly Further to my letters dated 11, 20, 25 and 28 January, 1, 4, 8, 23 and 24 February and 3 March 2005 (A/59/668 and Add.1-9), I have the honour to inform you that Afghanistan and Solomon Islands have made the payments necessary to reduce their arrears below the amount specified in Article 19 of the Charter of the United Nations.
