UNITED NATIONS Distr. GENERAL S/1998/420 22 May 1998 ORIGINAL: ENGLISH REPORT BY THE SECRETARY-GENERAL CONCERNING THE CREDENTIALS OF THE REPRESENTATIVE OF COSTA RICA ON THE SECURITY COUNCIL Pursuant to rule 15 of the provisional rules of procedure of the Security Council, the Secretary-General wishes to report that he has received credentials signed on 11 May 1998 by the President of the Republic of Costa Rica stating that Mr. Bernd Niehaus Quesada has been appointed representative of Costa Rica on the Security Council. In the opinion of the Secretary-General, these credentials are in order.
