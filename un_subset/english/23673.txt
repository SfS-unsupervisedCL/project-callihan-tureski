Original: COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS Fifty-ninth session Item 21 of the provisional agenda COMPREHENSIVE IMPLEMENTATION OF AND FOLLOW-UP TO THE DURBAN DECLARATION AND PROGRAMME OF ACTION Note verbale dated 22 January 2003 from the Permanent Mission of Brazil to the United Nations Office at Geneva addressed to the Office of the High Commissioner for Human Rights The Permanent Mission of Brazil to the United Nations Office and other International Organizations in Geneva presents its compliments to the Office of the High Commissioner for Human Rights and has the honour to request that the text below,* concerning the implementation of the Durban Declaration and Programme of Action, be circulated as an official document of the fifty-ninth session of the Commission on Human Rights. pages 2 to 4 for offset.
