Letter dated 12 September 2002 from the President of the Security Council addressed to the Secretary-General I have the honour to inform you that your letter dated 10 September 2002 (S/2002/1026) concerning your intention to appoint Ibrahim Gambari (Nigeria) as your Special Representative for Angola has been brought to the attention of the members of the Security Council. They take note of the information and intention contained in your letter. (Signed) Stefan Tafrov President of the Security Council
