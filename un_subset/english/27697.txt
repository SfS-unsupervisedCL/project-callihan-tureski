Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 2 December 2004 [on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/59/521)] 59/52. Observer status for the Organisation of Eastern Caribbean States in the General Assembly The General Assembly, Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Organisation of Eastern Caribbean States, 1. Decides to invite the Organisation of Eastern Caribbean States to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer; 2. Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution. 65th plenary meeting 2 December 2004
