Letter dated 25 October 2002 from the Secretary-General addressed to the President of the Security Council I have the honour to refer to resolution 1439 (2002), adopted by the Security Council on 18 October 2002, extending the mandate of the monitoring mechanism established pursuant to resolution 1295 (2000) for a further period of two months, ending on 19 December 2002, subject to review. In paragraph 5 of the resolution, the Council requests me, upon the adoption of the resolution and acting in consultation with the Committee established pursuant to resolution 864 (1993) on the situation in Angola, to appoint two experts to serve on the monitoring mechanism. Accordingly, following consultation with the Committee, I wish to inform you that I have appointed the following two experts: Ambassador Juan Larrain (Chile) Mr. Ismaila Seck (Senegal) I have also requested Ambassador Juan Larrain to continue to chair the monitoring mechanism. (Signed) Kofi A. Annan
