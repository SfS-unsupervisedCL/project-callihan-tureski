Sixty-sixth session Fifth Committee Agenda item 146 Administrative and budgetary aspects of the financing of the United Nations peacekeeping operations Financing of the support account for peacekeeping operations and the United Nations Logistics Base at Brindisi, Italy Note by the Secretary-General 1. The present note is issued in accordance with the prorating procedures approved by the General Assembly in its resolution 50/221 B. The annex to the note reflects the resources to be approved by the General Assembly in respect of each peacekeeping mission, including the prorated shares of the support account and of the United Nations Logistics Base at Brindisi, Italy. 2. Once the General Assembly has taken action on the recommendations of the Fifth Committee, a note will be issued on the approved level of resources for all peacekeeping operations. 3. The Fifth Committee is requested to take note of the present note.
