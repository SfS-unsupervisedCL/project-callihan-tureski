UNITED NATIONS Distr. GENERAL A/53/78 9 March 1998 ENGLISH ORIGINAL: SPANISH Fifty-third session Item 72 of the preliminary listA/53/50. GENERAL AND COMPLETE DISARMAMENT Letter dated 5 March 1998 from the Permanent Representative of Mexico to the United Nations addressed to the Secretary-General I have the honour to transmit herewith a copy of the Inter-American Convention against the Illicit Manufacturing of and Trafficking in Firearms, Ammunition, Explosives, and Other Related Materials, which was adopted and opened for signature by resolution AG/RES.1 (XXIV-E/97) at the twenty-fourth special session of the General Assembly of the Organization of American States, held on 13 and 14 November 1997 in Washington, D.C. (see annex). I should be grateful if this letter and its annex could be circulated as an official document of the General Assembly under item 72 of the preliminary list.
