Preparatory Committee for the United Nations Conference on the Illicit Trade in Small Arms and Light Weapons in All Its Aspects First session 28 February-3 March 2000 Note verbale dated 25 February 2000 from the Permanent Mission of Portugal to the United Nations The Permanent Mission of Portugal to the United Nations present its compliments to the secretariat of the first session of the Preparatory Committee for the United Nations Conference on the Illicit Trade in Small Arms and Light Weapons in All Its Aspects and, on behalf of the European Union and in pursuit of paragraph 9 of General Assembly resolution 54/54 V of 15 December 1999, has the honour to transmit as documents of the above-mentioned Preparatory Committee, the European Union Programme for Preventing and Combating Illicit Trafficking in Conventional Arms (annex I), and the European Union Code of Conduct (annex II).
