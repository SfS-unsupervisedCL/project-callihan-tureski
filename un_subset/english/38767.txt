Letter dated 17 October 2006 from the President of the Security Council addressed to the Secretary-General I have the honour to inform you that your letter dated 28 September 2006 (S/2006/778) indicating your intention to continue the activities of the United Nations support team for the Cameroon-Nigeria Mixed Commission with funding from the regular budget has been brought to the attention of the members of the Security Council. The members of the Security Council take note of the information contained in your letter and the intention expressed therein. At the same time, the Council requests further information and clarification in writing on the activities expected to be performed by the Mixed Commission in 2007 and following years and on those activities to be performed by the United Nations team. The members of the Security Council also urge the parties to the Mixed Commission to work with international donors to seek further voluntary contributions. (Signed) Kenzo Oshima President of the Security Council
