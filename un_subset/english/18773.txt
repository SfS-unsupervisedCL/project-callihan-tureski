Original: COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS Fifty-eighth session 18 March-26 April 2002 Item 2 of the provisional agenda ANNOTATIONS TO THE PROVISIONAL AGENDA Prepared by the Secretary-General Addendum 1. The present document has been prepared to complete the annotations to the provisional agenda of the Commission on Human Right at its fifty-eighth session (E/CN.4/2002/1/Add.1). 2. Its purpose is to bring to the attention of the Commission those resolutions adopted by the General Assembly at its fifty-sixth session which are considered relevant to the agenda items of this session of the Commission.* A list of those resolutions is reproduced hereafter. * At the time of preparation of the present document, the General Assembly at its fifty-sixth session has not yet taken action on the Durban Declaration and Programme of Action and related matters.
