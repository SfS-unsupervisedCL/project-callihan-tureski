Report by the Secretary-General concerning the credentials of the alternate representatives of Mexico on the Security Council Pursuant to rule 15 of the provisional rules of procedure of the Security Council, the Secretary-General wishes to report that he has received a note verbale dated 1 July 2003 from the Permanent Mission of Mexico to the United Nations stating that Mr. José Ramón Lorenzo, Ms. Amparo Anguiano, Mr. Héctor Peralta, Mr. Carlos Valera, Ms. Claudia Velasco, Mr. Alfonso Ascencio, Mr. Arturo Ponce, Mr. Diego Simancas and Ms. Mariana Olivera have been appointed alternate representatives of Mexico on the Security Council. In the opinion of the Secretary-General, that communication constitutes adequate provisional credentials.
