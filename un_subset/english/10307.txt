UNITED NATIONS Distr. GENERAL S/1999/827 28 July 1999 ENGLISH ORIGINAL: ARABIC LETTER DATED 27 JULY 1999 FROM THE CHARGÉ D'AFFAIRES A.I. OF THE PERMANENT MISSION OF THE LIBYAN ARAB JAMAHIRIYA TO THE UNITED NATIONS ADDRESSED TO THE PRESIDENT OF THE SECURITY COUNCIL I have the honour to transmit to you the text of decision AHG/Dec.3 (XXXV) Rev.3 (document CM/2102 (LXX)) concerning the crisis between the Libyan Arab Jamahiriya and the United States of America and the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland adopted by the Assembly of Heads of State and Government of the Organization of African Unity at its thirty-fifth ordinary session held at Algiers from 12 to 14 July 1999. I should be grateful if you would have the text of the decision distributed as a document of the Security Council. (Signed) Isa Ayad BABAA Chargé d'affaires a.i.
