Fifty-fifth session * A/55/150 and Corr.1 and 2. Item 17 (b) of the provisional agenda* Appointments to fill vacancies in subsidiary organs and other appointments Appointment of members of the Committee on Contributions Note by the Secretary-General Addendum The Secretary-General has received notification of the resignation of Ju Kuilin (China) from membership of the Committee on Contributions. Accordingly, the General Assembly will be required at its current session to appoint a person to fill the remainder of the term of office of Mr. Ju, which expires on 31 December 2002.
