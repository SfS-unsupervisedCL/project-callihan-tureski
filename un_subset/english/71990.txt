Letter dated 24 April 2014 from the Secretary-General addressed to the President of the Security Council In accordance with article 17, paragraph 3, of the Relationship Agreement between the United Nations and the International Criminal Court, I have the honour to convey the attached communication dated 11 April 2014 from the President of the Court (see annex). The President transmits the decision on the cooperation of the Democratic Republic of the Congo regarding Omar Al Bashir's arrest and surrender to the Court, issued by Pre-Trial Chamber II of the Court on 9 April 2014 in the case of the Prosecutor v. Omar Hassan Ahmad Al Bashir. I should be most grateful if you would bring the present letter and its annex to the attention of the members of the Security Council. (Signed) BAN Ki-moon Letter dated 11 April 2014 from the President of the International Criminal Court addressed to the Secretary-General Pursuant to article 17 (3) of the Relationship Agreement between the United Nations and the International Criminal Court and in accordance with article 87 (7) of the Rome Statute and regulation 109 (4) of the Regulations of the Court, and as requested by Pre-Trial Chamber II, I hereby transmit the decision on the cooperation of the Democratic Republic of the Congo regarding Omar Al Bashir's arrest and surrender to the Court of the Chamber, dated 9 April 2014, and refer this matter to the Security Council. I would be grateful if you would convey the present letter and its enclosure to the Security Council. (Signed) Sang-Hyun Song
