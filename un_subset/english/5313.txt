GENERAL S/1998/323 14 April 1998 ORIGINAL: ENGLISH LETTER DATED 14 APRIL 1998 FROM THE PRESIDENT OF THE SECURITY COUNCIL ADDRESSED TO THE SECRETARY-GENERAL I have the honour to inform you that your letter dated 8 April 1998 (S/1998/322) concerning your intention to add the Netherlands to the list of Member States contributing military personnel to the United Nations Peacekeeping Force in Cyprus has been brought to the attention of the members of the Security Council. They agree with the intention contained in your letter. (Signed) Hisashi OWADA President of the Security Council
