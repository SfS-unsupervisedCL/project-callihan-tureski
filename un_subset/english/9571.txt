UNITED NATIONS Distr. GENERAL S/1999/1206 29 November 1999 ORIGINAL: ENGLISH LETTER DATED 29 NOVEMBER 1999 FROM THE PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVE OF DJIBOUTI TO THE UNITED NATIONS ADDRESSED TO THE PRESIDENT OF THE SECURITY COUNCIL I have the honour to transmit to you herewith the declaration of the seventh Summit of Heads of State and Government of the Intergovernmental Authority on Development, held in Djibouti on 26 November 1999 (annex I) and the resolution on the Somali peace initiative and proposals of the President of the Republic of Djibouti adopted at the Summit (annex II). I should be grateful if you would have the text of the present letter and its annexes circulated as a document of the Security Council. (Signed) Roble OLHAYE Ambassador Permanent Representative English Page Annex I Declaration issued on 26 November 1999 in Djibouti by the seventh Summit of Heads of State and Government of the Intergovernmental Authority on Development Annex II Resolution on the Somali peace initiative and proposals of the President of Djibouti adopted on 26 November 1999 at the seventh Summit of Heads of State and Government of the Intergovernmental Authority on Development
