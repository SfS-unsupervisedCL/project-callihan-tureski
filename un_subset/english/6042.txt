UNITED NATIONS Distr. GENERAL S/AC.35/1998/30 22 May 1998 ENGLISH ORIGINAL: SPANISH SECURITY COUNCIL COMMITTEE ESTABLISHED PURSUANT TO RESOLUTION 1160 (1998) Note verbale dated 20 May 1998 from the Permanent Mission of Spain to the United Nations addressed to the Chairman of the Committee The Permanent Mission of Spain to the United Nations presents its compliments to the Chairman of the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1160 (1998) of 31 March 1998 and has the honour to inform him, in accordance with paragraph 12 of that resolution, that the Government of Spain has taken the necessary steps to give effect to the provisions of paragraph 8 of resolution 1160 (1998).
