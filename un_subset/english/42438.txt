Ninth United Nations Conference on the Standardization of Geographical Names * E/CONF.98/1. ** Prepared by China. *** The full text of the paper is being issued in English only as document E/CONF.98/62/Add.1. New York, 21-30 August 2007 Item 5 of the provisional agenda* Reports by divisions of the United Nations Group of Experts on Geographical Names on the situation in their divisions and on the progress made in the standardization of geographical names since the Eighth Conference Report of the China Division Submitted by the China Division** Summary*** The Public Services Project of Geographical Names has been carried out. Since the Eighth United Nations Conference on the Standardization of Geographical Names, progress has been made in the China Division in the establishment of the geographical names database, in the setting up of geographical name signs and in other areas.
