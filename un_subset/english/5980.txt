UNITED NATIONS Distr. GENERAL S/AC.31/1998/26 7 July 1998 ORIGINAL: ENGLISH SECURITY COUNCIL COMMITTEE ESTABLISHED PURSUANT TO RESOLUTION 864 (1993) CONCERNING THE SITUATION IN ANGOLA NOTE VERBALE DATED 2 JULY 1998 FROM THE PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVE OF HUNGARY TO THE UNITED NATIONS ADDRESSED TO THE CHAIRMAN OF THE COMMITTEE The Permanent Representative of the Republic of Hungary to the United Nations presents his compliments to the Chairman of the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 864 (1993) concerning the situation in Angola and has the honour to inform him that the Republic of Hungary has taken the necessary measures for the full implementation of the relevant provisions of Security Council resolution 1173 (1998) of 12 June 1998.
