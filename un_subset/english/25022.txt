%%%SPECIAL MISSIONS|KOSOVO (SERBIA)|SERBIA AND MONTENEGRO|APPOINTMENT OF OFFICIALS|

Letter dated 24 July 2003 from the Secretary-General addressed to the President of the Security Council I have the honour to inform the members of the Security Council that it is my intention to appoint Harri Holkeri (Finland) as my Special Representative and Head of the United Nations Interim Administration Mission in Kosovo (UNMIK). Mr. Holkeri will succeed Michael Steiner who has been reassigned by his Government to other duties. I should be grateful if you would bring this matter to the attention of the members of the Security Council. (Signed) Kofi A. Annan
