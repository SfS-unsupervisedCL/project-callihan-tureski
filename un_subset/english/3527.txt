98-29280 (E) 051098 United Nations A/C.4/53/3 General Assembly Distr.: General 5 October 1998 Original: English Fifty-third session Special Political and Decolonization Committee (Fourth Committee) Agenda item 18 Implementation of the Declaration on the Granting of Independence to Colonial Countries and Peoples Question of the United States Virgin Islands Request for hearing Letter dated 30 September 1998 from Mr. Carlyle Corbin, Representative for External Affairs, Government of the United States Virgin Islands, addressed to the Chairman of the Special Political and Decolonization Committee Distributed in accordance with a decision taken by the Special Political and Decolonization Committee at its 3rd meeting, on 5 October 1998. (Signed) Carlyle Corbin Representative for External Affairs, Government of the United States Virgin Islands
