Committee on the Peaceful Uses of Outer Space National legislation and practice relating to the definition and delimitation of outer space Note by the Secretariat Addendum Contents Page Replies received from Member States Norway United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland [Original: English] [23 November 2010] At present, Norway does not have any legislation or practices related to the definition and/or delimitation of outer space. Legislation and practice of the United Kingdom do not define or delimit space.
