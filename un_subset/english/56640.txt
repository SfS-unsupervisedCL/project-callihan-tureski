Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1737 (2006) Note verbale dated 11 August 2010 from the Permanent Mission of Brazil to the United Nations addressed to the Chairman of the Committee * Reissued for technical reasons on 22 September 2010. ** Decree 7.259 is on file with the Secretariat and is available for consultation. The Permanent Mission of Brazil to the United Nations presents its compliments to the Chairman of the Committee and has the honour to inform the Committee that, on 10 August 2010, the President of the Federative Republic of Brazil, Luiz In√°cio Lula da Silva, signed Decree 7.259/2010,** which incorporates the provisions of resolution 1929 (2010) into Brazilian law and determines that all competent Brazilian authorities must enforce them. The Permanent Mission of Brazil would also like to advise that, as previously communicated to the Committee in national reports S/AC.50/2007/26, S/AC.50/2007/82 and S/AC.50/2008/63, Security Council resolutions 1737 (2006), 1747 (2007), 1803 (2008) and 1835 (2008) were incorporated into Brazilian law through Decrees 6.045/2007, 6.118/2007, 6.448/2008 and 6.735/2009, respectively. Detailed information with regard to Brazil's national implementation of Security Council sanctions can be found in the national report of Brazil contained in document S/AC.49/2010/7, submitted to the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) on 26 May 2010.
