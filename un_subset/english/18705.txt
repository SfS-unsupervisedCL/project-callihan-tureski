UNITED NATIONS E Economic and Social Council Distr. Original: COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS Fifty-eighth session Item 19 of the provisional agenda advisory services and technical cooperation in the field of human rights Technical cooperation and the situation of human rights in Haiti Note by the secretariat 1. In the statement made by the Chairperson of the Commission on 25 April 2001 at its fiftyseventh session (E/2001/23-E/CN.4/2001/167, para. 604) concerning technical cooperation and the situation of human rights in Haiti, the Commission on Human Rights requested a new independent expert on the situation of human rights in Haiti to report to the General Assembly at its fifty-sixth session and to the Commission at its fifty-eighth session on developments in the situation of human rights and technical cooperation for human rights in Haiti. 2. Owing to the fact that a new independent expert on the situation of human rights in Haiti has not yet been appointed, no report has been submitted. GE.02-11479 (E) 210302
