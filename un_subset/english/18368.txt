CONFERENCE ON DISARMAMENT CD/1669 10 May 2002 Original: ENGLISH LETTER DATED 6 MAY 2002 FROM THE PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVE OF SOUTH AFRICA TO THE CONFERENCE ON DISARMAMENT ADDRESSED TO THE SECRETARY-GENERAL OF THE CONFERENCE TRANSMITTING AN EXTRACT OF PARAGRAPHS RELATED TO DISARMAMENT AND INTERNATIONAL SECURITY FROM THE COMMUNIQUÉ OF THE MINISTERIAL MEETING OF THE COORDINATING BUREAU OF THE NON-ALIGNED MOVEMENT, HELD IN DURBAN ON 29 APRIL 2002 I have the honour to transmit herewith an extract of paragraphs issued in relation to Disarmament and International Security, from the Communiqué of the Ministerial Meeting of the Co-ordinating Bureau of the Non-Aligned Movement held in Durban on 29 April 2002. I would be grateful if this document could be issued and circulated as an official document of the Conference on Disarmament to the delegations of all member States of the Conference and non-member States participating in its work. (Signed:) Sipho George Nene Ambassador Permanent Representative to the Conference on Disarmament
