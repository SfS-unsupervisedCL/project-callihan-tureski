Summary statement by the Secretary-General on matters of which the Security Council is seized and on the stage reached in their consideration Addendum Pursuant to rule 11 of the provisional rules of procedure of the Security Council, the Secretary-General is submitting the following summary statement. The list of items of which the Security Council is seized is contained in documents S/2002/30 of 15 March 2002, S/2002/30/Add.1 of 22 March 2002, S/2002/30/Add.2 of 25 March 2002, S/2002/30/Add.3 of 26 March 2002, S/2002/30/Add.5 of 1 April 2002, S/2002/30/Add.13 of 9 April 2002, S/2002/30/Add.23 of 21 June 2002 and S/2002/30/Add.27 of 19 July 2002. During the week ending 24 August 2002, the Security Council took no action on any of the items listed therein.
