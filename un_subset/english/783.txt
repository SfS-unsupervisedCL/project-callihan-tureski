UNITED NATIONS Distr. GENERAL A/ES-10/18 31 October 1997 ORIGINAL: ENGLISH Tenth emergency special session Agenda item 5 ILLEGAL ISRAELI ACTIONS IN OCCUPIED EAST JERUSALEM AND THE REST OF THE OCCUPIED PALESTINIAN TERRITORY Letter dated 30 October 1997 from the Permanent Representative of Indonesia to the United Nations addressed to the President of the General Assembly In my capacity as Chairman of the Islamic Group of the Organization of the Islamic Conference in New York, I have the honour to convey to Your Excellency the Islamic Group's support to the request submitted to Your Excellency by the Permanent Representative of the Republic of Yemen, as Chairman of the Arab Group for the month of October 1997, for the resumption of the tenth emergency special session of the General Assembly to consider the "Illegal Israeli actions in occupied East Jerusalem and the rest of the Occupied Palestinian Territory" in the light of the report of the Secretary-General submitted in accordance with General Assembly resolution ES-10/3 (A/ES-10/16). I would appreciate it if you would have the text of the present letter circulated as a document of the tenth emergency special session of the General Assembly. (Signed) Makarim WIBISONO Ambassador Permanent Representative Chairman of the Islamic Group
