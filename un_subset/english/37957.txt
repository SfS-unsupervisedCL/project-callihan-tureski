Commission on Narcotic Drugs Forty-ninth session Vienna,13-17 March 2006 Item 10 of the provisional agenda* * E/CN.7/2006/1. Administrative and budgetary questions Proposed strategic framework for the biennium 2008-2009 Note by the Secretariat Pursuant to paragraph 32 of the report of the Secretary-General, entitled "Implementation of decisions from the 2005 World Summit Outcome for action by the Secretary-General" (A/60/430), the preparation of the proposed strategic framework for the biennium 2008-2009 will be initiated by the Secretary-General subsequent to the General Assembly's approval of the review of all mandates older than five years, as requested in paragraph 163 (b) of the 2005 World Summit Outcome (Assembly resolution 60/1). Consequently, the proposed strategic framework will be submitted to the Committee for Programme and Coordination in August/September 2006 so that its recommendations may be presented to the Assembly at its sixty-first session.
