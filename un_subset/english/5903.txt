GENERAL S/1998/95 3 February 1998 ORIGINAL: ENGLISH LETTER DATED 2 FEBRUARY 1998 FROM THE PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVE OF THE SUDAN TO THE UNITED NATIONS ADDRESSED TO THE SECRETARY-GENERAL Upon instructions from my Government, I have the honour to inform you that the Government of the Republic of the Sudan wishes to retain item 4 of paragraph 7 of the summary statement by the Secretary-General on matters of which the Security Council is seized and on the stage reached in their consideration (S/1998/44), entitled "Letter dated 20 February 1958 from the representative of the Sudan addressed to the Secretary-General (S/3963)", on the list of matters of which the Security Council is seized. I would be grateful if you could have the present letter circulated as a document of the Security Council. (Signed) Elfatih ERWA Permanent Representative
