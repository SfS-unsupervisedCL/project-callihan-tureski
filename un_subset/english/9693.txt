UNITED NATIONS Distr. GENERAL S/1999/168 18 February 1999 ORIGINAL: ENGLISH NOTE BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE SECURITY COUNCIL The Security Council today considered and approved the report of the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 864 (1993) concerning the situation in Angola contained in document S/1999/147 of 12 February 1999. The Council endorsed the recommendations contained in the report.
