Fifty-ninth session Agenda item 17 (b) Appointments to fill vacancies in subsidiary organs and other appointments Appointment of a member of the Committee on Contributions Note by the Secretary-General Addendum The Secretary-General has received notification of the resignation of Alvaro Gurgel de Alencar Netto (Brazil) from the Committee on Contributions. Accordingly, the General Assembly will be required to appoint, at its current session, a person to fill the vacancy for the remaining period of the term of office of Mr. Gurgel de Alencar, which expires on 31 December 2005.
