Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) Note verbale dated 31 July 2009 from the Permanent Mission of Pakistan to the United Nations addressed to the Chair of the Committee The Permanent Mission of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan to the United Nations presents its compliments to the Chair of the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) and, in continuation of its note verbale dated 27 July 2009, has the honour to transmit herewith the following additional information on the implementation of Security Council resolution 1874 (2009) in Pakistan (see annex). In pursuance to paragraph 24 of Security Council resolution 1874 (2009), the Government of Pakistan has issued Gazette Notification S.R.O. No. 690 (I)/2009 dated 27 July 2009, providing notification regarding the additional lists of entities, goods and individuals as designated by the Committee to whom all measures as set out in S.R.O. 1063 (I)/2006 dated 16 October 2006, S.R.O. 1289 (I)/2006 dated 7 November 2006 and S.R.O. 667 (I)/2009 dated 16 July 2009 will be applicable.
