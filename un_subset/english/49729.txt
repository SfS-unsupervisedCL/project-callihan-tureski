Tenth emergency special session Agenda item 5 Illegal Israeli actions in Occupied East Jerusalem and the rest of the Occupied Palestinian Territory Letter dated 8 January 2009 from the Permanent Representative of Indonesia to the United Nations addressed to the President of the General Assembly Upon the instruction of my Government, and as expressed in the statement of Indonesia at the resumed emergency meeting of the Security Council on 7 January 2008, I have the honour to request the resumption, at a time deemed appropriate, of the tenth emergency special session of the General Assembly to consider the situation in the Gaza Strip. (Signed) R. M. Marty M. Natalegawa
