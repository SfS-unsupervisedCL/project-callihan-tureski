%%%MIDDLE EAST SITUATION|PALESTINE QUESTION|

Letter dated 18 February 2011 from the Permanent Observer of Palestine to the United Nations addressed to the President of the Security Council I have the honour to request that, in accordance with its previous practice, the Security Council invite the Permanent Observer of Palestine to the United Nations to participate in the Security Council meeting which will be held on Friday, 18 February 2011, regarding the situation in the Middle East, including the Palestinian question. (Signed) Riyad Mansour Ambassador Permanent Observer
