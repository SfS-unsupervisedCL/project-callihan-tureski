Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1737 (2006) Note verbale dated 28 April 2008 from the Permanent Mission of Argentina to the United Nations addressed to the Chairman of the Committee * The text of the legislation is on file with the Secretariat and is available for consultation. The Permanent Mission of the Argentine Republic to the United Nations presents its compliments to the Office of the Chairman of the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1737 (2006) and has the honour to enclose herewith a copy of Ministry of Foreign Affairs, International Trade and Worship resolution 617/2008,* which promulgates the sanctions regime imposed on the Islamic Republic of Iran through Security Council resolutions 1737 (2006) and 1747 (2007).
