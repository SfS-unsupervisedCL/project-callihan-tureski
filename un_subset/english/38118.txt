Summary statement by the Secretary-General on matters of which the Security Council is seized and on the stage reached in their consideration Addendum Pursuant to rule 11 of the provisional rules of procedure of the Security Council, the Secretary-General is submitting the following summary statement. The list of items of which the Security Council is seized is contained in documents S/2006/10 of 1 March 2006 and S/2006/10/Add.12 of 7 April 2006. During the week ending 15 April 2006, the Security Council took action on the following items: International Tribunal for the Prosecution of Persons Responsible for Serious Violations of International Humanitarian Law Committed in the Territory of the Former Yugoslavia since 1991 (see S/1996/15/Add.18; S/1998/44/Add.19, 34 and 46; S/2000/40/Add.24; S/2001/15/Add.3, 6 and 17; S/2002/30/Add.29; S/2003/40/Add.20; S/2004/20/Add.41; S/2005/15/Add.2, 10, 15, 22, 29 and 38; and S/2006/10/Add.8) The Security Council resumed its consideration of the item at its 5407th meeting, held on 10 April 2006 in accordance with the understanding reached in its prior consultations, having before it a letter dated 27 March 2006 from the Secretary-General addressed to the President of the Security Council (S/2006/199). The President drew attention to a draft resolution (S/2006/223) that had been prepared in the course of the Council's prior consultations. The Security Council proceeded to vote on draft resolution S/2006/223, and adopted it unanimously as resolution 1668 (2006) (for the text, see S/RES/1668 (2006); to be issued in Official Records of the Security Council, Resolutions and Decisions of the Security Council, 1 August 2005-31 July 2006). see S/1997/40/Add.21; S/1998/44/Add.35 and 49; S/1999/25/Add.10, 13, 24, 30, 43, 47 and 49; S/2000/40/Add.3, 7, 16, 17, 19, 21, 23, 30, 33, 40, 47 and 49; S/2001/15/Add.5, 8, 18, 24, 30, 35, 36, 43, 45, 50 and 51; S/2002/30/Add.4, 8, 11, 20, 22, 23, 29, 31, 32, 36, 41, 42, 44 and 48; S/2003/40/Add.3, 6, 11, 19, 21, 25, 27, 28, 30, 32, 34 and 46; S/2004/20/Add.2, 10, 19, 23, 25, 30, 39 and 49; S/2005/15/Add.8, 12, 14, 15, 25, 27, 29, 35, 38, 39, 42 and 50; and S/2006/10/Add.3 and 4) The Security Council resumed its consideration of the item at its 5408th meeting, held on 10 April 2006 in accordance with the understanding reached in its prior consultations, having before it a letter dated 30 March 2006 from the Secretary-General addressed to the President of the Security Council (S/2006/206). The President, with the consent of the Council, invited the representative of the Democratic Republic of the Congo, at his request, to participate in the consideration of the item without the right to vote. The President drew attention to a draft resolution (S/2006/224) that had been prepared in the course of the Council's prior consultations. The Security Council proceeded to vote on draft resolution S/2006/224, and adopted it unanimously as resolution 1669 (2006) (for the text, see S/RES/1669 (2006); to be issued in Official Records of the Security Council, Resolutions and Decisions of the Security Council, 1 August 2005-31 July 2006). Reports of the Secretary-General on the Sudan (see S/2004/20/Add.23, 30, 35, 37, 39, 40, 44, 46 and 49; S/2005/15/Add.1, 4-6, 9-12, 18, 25, 28, 30, 37, 40, 49 and 50; and S/2006/10/Add.1, 4, 11 and 12) The Security Council resumed its consideration of the item at its 5409th meeting, held on 11 April 2006 in accordance with the understanding reached in its prior consultations. The President stated that, following consultations of the Council, he had been authorized to make a statement on behalf of the Council and read out the text of that statement (for the text, see S/PRST/2006/16; to be issued in Official Records of the Security Council, Resolutions and Decisions of the Security Council, 1 August 2005-31 July 2006). The situation between Eritrea and Ethiopia (see S/1998/44/Add.25; S/1999/25/Add.3, 5 and 7; S/2000/40/Add.18, 19, 30, 32, 36, 45 and 46; S/2001/15/Add.6, 11, 16, 20, 37 and 46; S/2002/30/Add.2, 9, 10, 19, 32 and 35; S/2003/40/Add.10, 28 and 36; S/2004/20/Add.10 and 37; S/2005/15/Add.10, 36, 39, 46, 48 and 49; and S/2006/10/Add.7 and 10) The Security Council resumed its consideration of the item at its 5410th meeting, held on 13 April 2006 in accordance with the understanding reached in its prior consultations, having before it the report of the Secretary-General on Ethiopia and Eritrea (S/2006/140). The President drew attention to a draft resolution (S/2006/232) that had been prepared in the course of the Council's prior consultations. The Security Council proceeded to vote on draft resolution S/2006/232, and adopted it unanimously as resolution 1670 (2006) (for the text, see S/RES/1670 (2006); to be issued in Official Records of the Security Council, Resolutions and Decisions of the Security Council, 1 August 2005-31 July 2006).
