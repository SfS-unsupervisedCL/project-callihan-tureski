99-23522 (E) PCNICC/1999/WGEC/DP.27 12 August 1999 English Original: English and Russian Preparatory Commission for the International Criminal Court Working Group on Elements of Crimes New York 16–26 February 1999 26 July–13 August 1999 29 November–17 December 1999 Proposal submitted by China and the Russian Federation on the elements of article 8 (2) (c) (I) in the discussion paper proposed by the Coordinator (PCNICC/1999/WGEC/RT.5/Rev.1) Add the following as element 5 to article 8 (2) (c) (I)-4: War crime of torture: “5. The conduct was instigated or committed with the consent or acquiescence of a public official or other person acting in an official capacity, including persons who have been provided with similar powers or capacity by an adverse party, irrespective of whether the party was recognized as a State.”
