Letter dated 16 October 2013 from the President of the Security Council addressed to the Secretary-General I have the honour to inform you that your letter dated 13 October 2013 (S/2013/608) concerning your intention to appoint Ms. Sigrid Kaag (Netherlands) to the post of Special Coordinator of the OPCW-United Nations Joint Mission has been brought to the attention of the members of the Security Council. They take note of the intention expressed in your letter. (Signed) Agshin Mehdiyev President of the Security Council
