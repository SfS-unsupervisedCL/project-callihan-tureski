%%%PEACEBUILDING|TAJIKISTAN|TAJIKISTAN SITUATION|

Letter dated 31 May 2006 from the President of the Security Council addressed to the Secretary-General I have the honour to inform you that your letter dated 26 May 2006 (S/2006/355) regarding your intention to continue the peacekeeping activities of the United Nations Tajikistan Office of Peacebuilding for a further one-year period, until 1 June 2007, has been brought to the attention of the members of the Security Council. They have taken note of the information and the intention conveyed in your letter. (Signed) Basile Ikouebe President of the Security Council
