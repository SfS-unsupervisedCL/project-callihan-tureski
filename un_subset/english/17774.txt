Items 9 and 10 of the provisional agenda* Ministerial segment Summit segment of the provisional agenda Letter dated 5 March 2002 from the Chargé d'affaires of the Permanent Mission of Mexico to the United Nations and the Permanent Representative of Norway to the United Nations addressed to the Secretary-General The Governments of Mexico and Norway, the Inter-American Development Bank and the Group of 24 co-sponsored a conference on the theme "Financing for development: regional challenges and the regional development banks" and a round table on the theme "New proposals on financing for development" in Washington, D.C., on 19 and 20 February 2002. The conference and round table were organized by the Centre for Global Development and the Institute for International Economics in support of the International Conference on Financing for Development, to be held in Monterrey, Mexico, from 18 to 22 March 2002. We have the honour to transmit the attached Chairperson's report of the outcomes of the conference and round table (see annex). The report has been prepared by the Chairpersons of the two events. It does not necessarily represent the views of the Mexican and Norwegian Governments. Upon instruction from our Governments, we have the honour to request that the present letter and its annex be circulated as a document of the International Conference on Financing for Development. (Signed) Roberta Lajous Ambassador Chargé d'affaires a.i. Permanent Mission of Mexico to the United Nations (Signed) Ole Peter Kolby Permanent Representative of Norway to the United Nations * A/CONF.198/1.
