Letter dated 23 March 2011 from the Permanent Representative of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland to the United Nations addressed to the Secretary-General I have the honour to refer to Security Council resolution 1973 (2011) and to my letter of 19 March 2011 notifying you of measures taken by the United Kingdom pursuant to the authorization contained in paragraphs 4 and 8 of the resolution. I hereby notify you of the following additional measures taken since 19 March 2011 under those authorizations, including: :: United Kingdom aircraft have been deployed in Libyan airspace to patrol the no-fly zone established pursuant to paragraph 6 of resolution 1973 (2011); :: United Kingdom aircraft have also been deployed in Libyan airspace to conduct reconnaissance missions in order to protect the civilian population from attacks by Libyan forces. I would be grateful if you would circulate the present letter as a document of the Security Council. (Signed) Mark Lyall Grant
