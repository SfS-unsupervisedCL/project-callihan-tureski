Item 60 of the preliminary list* Maintenance of international security -- good-neighbourliness, stability and development in South-Eastern Europe Letter dated 25 May 2004 from the Chargé d'affaires a.i. of the Permanent Mission of the former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia to the United Nations addressed to the Secretary-General I have the honour, on my own behalf and on behalf of the Permanent Representatives of Croatia, Mr. Vladimir Drobnjak, and of Albania, Mr. Agim Nesho, to draw your attention to the joint statement of the representatives of Croatia, Albania, the Republic of Macedonia and the United States of America on the occasion of the second meeting of the Partnership Commission (see annex).
