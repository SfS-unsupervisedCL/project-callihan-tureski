%%%PEACEKEEPING OPERATIONS|DARFUR (SUDAN)|SUDAN|APPOINTMENT OF OFFICIALS|

Letter dated 24 July 2009 from the President of the Security Council to the Secretary-General I have the honour to inform you that your letter dated 22 July 2009 (S/2009/382) concerning your agreement with the Chairperson of the African Union Commission to appoint Lieutenant General Patrick Nyamvumba (Rwanda) as Force Commander of the African Union-United Nations Hybrid Operation in Darfur (UNAMID) has been brought to the attention of the members of the Security Council. They take note of the agreement expressed in your letter. (Signed) Ruhakana Rugunda President of the Security Council
