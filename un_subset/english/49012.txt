Sixty-fourth session Agenda item 171 Observer status in the General Assembly for the Council of Presidents of the General Assembly Report of the Sixth Committee Rapporteur: Mr. Jean-CÃ©dric Janssens de Bisthoven (Belgium) I. Introduction 1. The item entitled "Observer status in the General Assembly for the Council of Presidents of the General Assembly" was included in the agenda of the sixty-fourth session of the General Assembly at the request of Saint Lucia, Saudi Arabia and Ukraine. 2. At its 52nd plenary meeting, on 1 December 2009, the General Assembly, on the recommendation of the General Committee, decided to include the item in its agenda and to allocate it to the Sixth Committee. 3. The Sixth Committee considered the item at its 26th and 27th meetings, on 9 and 14 December 2009. The views of the representatives who spoke during the Committee's consideration of the item are reflected in the relevant summary records (A/C.6/64/SR.26 and 27). 4. For its consideration of the item, the Committee had before it a letter dated 12 November 2009 from the Permanent Representatives of Saint Lucia, Saudi Arabia and Ukraine to the United Nations addressed to the Secretary-General (A/64/235). II. Consideration of draft resolution A/C.6/64/L.20 5. At the 26th meeting, on 9 December, the representative of Saint Lucia, on behalf of Saint Lucia, Saudi Arabia and Ukraine, introduced a draft resolution entitled "Observer status in the General Assembly for the Council of Presidents of the General Assembly" (A/C.6/64/L.20). 6. At the 27th meeting, on 14 December, the representative of Saudi Arabia, on behalf of Saint Lucia, Saudi Arabia and Ukraine, withdrew draft resolution A/C.6/64/L.20. 7. At the same meeting, the Sixth Committee concluded its consideration of the item without taking action.
