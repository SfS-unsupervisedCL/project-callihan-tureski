Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 751 (1992) concerning Somalia Note verbale dated 12 March 2004 from the Permanent Mission of Albania to the United Nations addressed to the Chairman of the Committee The Permanent Mission of the Republic of Albania to the United Nations presents its compliments to the Chairman of the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 751 (1992) concerning Somalia and, with reference to the latter's note (SCA/1/04(03)), has the honour to inform him that the Republic of Albania has never had or entered into any agreement with Somalia in the field of the transfer, selling and buying of ammunition, armaments or other military materials. The Republic of Albania fully complies with the United Nations resolutions and ensures the full and effective implementation of the arms embargo against Somalia.
