%%%COUNTER-TERRORISM|TERRORISM|APPOINTMENT OF OFFICIALS|

Letter dated 23 December 2010 from the President of the Security Council to the Secretary-General I have the honour to inform you that your letter dated 20 December 2010 (S/2010/664) concerning your intention to extend the appointment of Mr. Mike Smith (Australia) as the Executive Director of the Counter-Terrorism Committee Executive Directorate through 31 December 2011 has been brought to the attention of the members of the Security Council, who have taken note thereof. (Signed) Susan E. Rice President of the Security Council
