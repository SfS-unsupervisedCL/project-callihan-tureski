Note verbale dated 16 April 2001 from the Permanent Mission of Angola to the United Nations addressed to the President of the Security Council The Permanent Mission of the Republic of Angola to the United Nations in its capacity as President of the African Countries Using Portuguese as an Official Language (PALOP) presents its compliments to the President of the Security Council and has the honour to transmit the final communiqué of the PALOP Summit, dated 10 April 2001, annexed hereto, and to request that this document be circulated as a document of the Security Council.
