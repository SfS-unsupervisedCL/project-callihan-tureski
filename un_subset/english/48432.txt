Sixty-third session Agenda item 132 * Reissued for technical reasons. Administrative and budgetary aspects of the financing of the United Nations peacekeeping operations Report of the Fifth Committee Rapporteur: Mr. Patrick A. Chuasoto (Philippines) I. Introduction 1. At its 2nd plenary meeting, on 19 September 2008, the General Assembly, on the recommendation of the General Committee, decided to include in the agenda of its sixty-third session the item entitled "Administrative and budgetary aspects of the financing of the United Nations peacekeeping operations" and to allocate it to the Fifth Committee. 2. The Fifth Committee considered the item at its 42nd, 45th, 48th, 52nd and 55th meetings, on 13, 18 and 26 May and 4 and 25 June 2009. Statements and observations made in the course of the Committee's consideration of the item are reflected in the relevant summary records (A/C.5/63/SR.42, 45, 48, 52 and 55). 3. For its consideration of the item, the Committee had before it the following documents: Cross-cutting issues (a) Report of the Secretary-General on peacekeeping best practices (A/62/593 and Corr.1); (b) Report of the Secretary-General on the overview of the financing of the United Nations peacekeeping operations: budget performance for the period from 1 July 2006 to 30 June 2007 and budget for the period from 1 July 2008 to 30 June 2009 (A/62/727); II. Consideration of proposals A. Draft resolution A/C.5/63/L.50 4. At its 55th meeting, on 25 June, the Committee had before it a draft resolution entitled "Rates of reimbursement to troop-contributing countries" (A/C.5/63/L.50), which was submitted by the Chairman on the basis of informal consultations coordinated by the representative of Austria. 5. At the same meeting, the Committee adopted draft resolution A/C.5/63/L.50 without a vote (see para. 14, draft resolution I). 6. After the adoption of the draft resolution, the representative of Guatemala made a statement. B. Draft resolution A/C.5/63/L.51 7. At its 55th meeting, on 25 June, the Committee had before it a draft resolution entitled "Financing of the United Nations Logistics Base at Brindisi, Italy" (A/C.5/63/L.51), which was submitted by the Chairman on the basis of informal consultations coordinated by the representative of Mexico. 8. At the same meeting, the Committee adopted draft resolution A/C.5/63/L.51 without a vote (see para. 14, draft resolution II). C. Draft resolution A/C.5/63/L.52 9. At its 55th meeting, on 25 June, the Committee had before it a draft resolution entitled "Support account for peacekeeping operations" (A/C.5/63/L.52), which was submitted by the Chairman on the basis of informal consultations coordinated by the representative of South Africa. 10. At the same meeting, the Committee adopted draft resolution A/C.5/63/L.52 without a vote (see para. 14, draft resolution III). 11. After the adoption of the draft resolution, the representative of the Sudan (on behalf of the Group of 77 and China) made a statement. D. Oral draft decision 12. At its 55th meeting, on 25 June, the Chairman of the Committee presented an oral draft decision entitled "Closed peacekeeping missions" reached by consensus and coordinated by the representative of Singapore. 13. At the same meeting, the Committee adopted the oral draft decision without a vote (see para. 15). 14.
