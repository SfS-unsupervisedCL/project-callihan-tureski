99-19471 (E) 130799 United Nations E/1999/60/Add.1 Economic and Social Council Distr.: General 30 June 1999 Original: English Substantive session of 1999 Geneva, 5–30 July 1999 Agenda item 6 Integrated and coordinated implementation of and follow-up to major United Nations conferences and summits Report of the Commission on the Status of Women acting as the preparatory committee for the special session of the General Assembly entitled “Women 2000: gender equality, development and peace for the twenty-first century” on its resumed second session Addendum 1. The Commission on the Status of Women acting as the preparatory committee for the special session of the General Assembly entitled “Women 2000: gender equality, development and peace for the twenty-first century” met in resumed session on 30 June 1999, in accordance with the provisions of Economic and Social Council decision 1999/213. 2. At that meeting, the Preparatory Committee elected Roselyn Ruth Asumwa Odera (Kenya) Chairperson by acclamation to replace Irma Engelbrecht (South Africa), who was unable to complete her term as Chairperson. 3. The Chairperson made a statement. 4. The Preparatory Committee also elected Asit Bhattacharjee (India) and Misako Kaji (Japan) as Vice-Chairpersons by acclamation to fill the two remaining vacancies on the Bureau.
