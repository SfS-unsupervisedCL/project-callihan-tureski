Letter dated 7 May 2002 from the President of the Security Council addressed to the Secretary-General I have the honour to inform you that your letter dated 2 May 2002 (S/2002/529) concerning your intention to appoint Sven Christian Frederiksen (Denmark) as Commissioner of the United Nations Mission in Bosnia and Herzegovina International Police Task Force has been brought to the attention of the members of the Security Council. They take note of the intention contained in your letter. (Signed) Kishore Mahbubani President of the Security Council
