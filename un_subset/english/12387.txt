UNITED NATIONS E Economic and Social Council Distr. Original: COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS Sub-Commission on the Promotion and Protection of Human Rights Fifty-second session Item 5 (b) of the provisional agenda THE IMPLEMENTATION OF HUMAN RIGHTS WITH REGARD TO WOMEN: THE ROLE AND EQUAL PARTICIPATION OF WOMEN IN DEVELOPMENT Note by the secretariat In resolution 1999/15, the Sub-Commission invited the SecretaryGeneral to continue to provide all available information on women and the right to development. As of 25 May 2000, the SecretaryGeneral was not in a position to provide the Sub-Commission with any information on this question. The SecretaryGeneral will, however, refer to Sub-Commission resolution 1999/15 and provide information on women and the right to development in the comprehensive report on the right to development he will submit to the General Assembly at its fifty-sixth session, as requested by the Commission on Human Rights in its resolution 2000/5.
