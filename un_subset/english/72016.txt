%%%APPOINTMENT OF OFFICIALS|WESTERN SAHARA|WESTERN SAHARA QUESTION|

Letter dated 8 May 2014 from the President of the Security Council addressed to the Secretary-General I have the honour to inform you that your letter dated 6 May 2014 (S/2014/322) concerning your intention to appoint Kim Bolduc (Canada) as your Special Representative for Western Sahara and Head of the United Nations Mission for the Referendum in Western Sahara has been brought to the attention of the members of the Security Council. They take note of the intention expressed in your letter. (Signed) Oh Joon President of the Security Council
