Thirteenth Meeting New York, 9-13 June 2003 Decision relating to the Financial Regulations of the International Tribunal for the Law of the Sea The Meeting of States Parties adopts the Financial Regulations of the International Tribunal for the Law of the Sea as contained in document SPLOS/2003/WP.3, and decides that the Regulations shall become effective on 1 January 2004 and shall apply to the financial period 2005-2006 and to subsequent financial periods.
