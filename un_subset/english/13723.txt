Fifty-fifth session Agenda item 122 Scale of assessments for the apportionment of the expenses of the United Nations Letter dated 13 February 2001 from the Secretary-General to the President of the General Assembly Addendum Further to my letter dated 24 January 2001 (A/55/745), I have the honour to inform you that Burkina Faso, Djibouti, Mongolia, Nicaragua and Turkmenistan have made the necessary payments to reduce their arrears below the amount specified in Article 19 of the Charter of the United Nations. (Signed) Kofi Annan Secretary-General
