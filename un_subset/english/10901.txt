On behalf of the Governments of the Arab Republic of Egypt, the Democratic People's Republic of Algeria and Portugal in their capacities as host of the Africa-European Union Summit, current Chairman of the Organization of African Unity and President of the European Union respectively, we have the honour to enclose herewith the Cairo Declaration and the Cairo Plan of Action adopted by the Africa-European Union Summit, held in Cairo on 3 and 4 April 2000 under the aegis of the Organization of African Unity and the European Union (see annexes).* We should be grateful if the present letter and its annexes could be circulated as a document of the General Assembly, under agenda items 24, 31, 35, 39, 46, 58, 70, 73, 76 (f), (m) and (o), 81, 97, 98 (a), 99 (a), (d), (e), (f) and (g), 100, 105, 108, 110, 111, 112, 114, 116 (a), 158, 160 and 165, and of the Economic and Social Council, under items 4, 5, 13 (a) and 14 (a), (b), (d), (e), (f) and (g) of the provisional agenda.
