Letter dated 8 February 2002 from the President of the Security Council to the Secretary-General I have the honour to inform you that your letter dated 4 February 2002 (S/2002/156), concerning your intention to take the administrative and financial measures necessary to facilitate the establishment and support of an integrated United Nations mission in Afghanistan, has been brought to the attention of the members of the Council. They take note of the intention stated in your letter, on the understanding that the interim administrative and financial measures taken will be without prejudice to final decisions on the financing of the mission. (Signed) Adolfo Aguilar Zinser
