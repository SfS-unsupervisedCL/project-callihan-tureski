UNITED NATIONS Distr. GENERAL S/AC.31/1999/5 19 February 1999 ENGLISH ORIGINAL: ARABIC SECURITY COUNCIL COMMITTEE ESTABLISHED PURSUANT TO RESOLUTION 864 (1993) CONCERNING THE SITUATION IN ANGOLA LETTER DATED 17 FEBRUARY 1999 FROM THE CHARGÉ D'AFFAIRES A.I. OF THE PERMANENT MISSION OF SAUDI ARABIA TO THE UNITED NATIONS ADDRESSED TO THE CHAIRMAN OF THE COMMITTEE I refer to your note dated 27 January 1999 (SCA/2/99(2)), in which you request information on the measures taken by the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to implement the provisions of Security Council resolutions 1127 (1997) and 1173 (1998) concerning the situation in Angola. I have the honour to inform you that the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is in compliance with the requirements of the aforesaid resolutions as they concern the União Nacional para a Independência Total de Angola (UNITA) and that it has taken no measures with regard to UNITA that conflict with those resolutions. I should be grateful if you would have this letter circulated as a document of the Security Council. (Signed) Abdulrahman S. AL-AHMED Chargé d'affaires a.i.
