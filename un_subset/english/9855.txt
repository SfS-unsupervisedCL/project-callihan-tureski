UNITED NATIONS Distr. GENERAL S/1999/302 21 March 1999 ORIGINAL: ENGLISH LETTER DATED 20 MARCH 1999 FROM THE CHARGÉ D'AFFAIRES A.I. OF THE PERMANENT MISSION OF YUGOSLAVIA TO THE UNITED NATIONS ADDRESSED TO THE PRESIDENT OF THE SECURITY COUNCIL I have the honour to forward, enclosed herewith, the Agreement for Self-Government in Kosmet (Kosovo and Metohija, the province of the Yugoslav constituent Republic of Serbia) that was signed by the delegation of the Government of the Republic of Serbia in Paris on 18 March 1999 (annex I), the statement of the Government of the Republic of Serbia of 19 March 1999 (annex II) and the statement by Milan Milutinovic, President of the Republic of Serbia of 18 March 1999 (annex III). I would be very grateful if you would have this letter and its annexes circulated as documents of the Security Council. (Signed) Vladislav JOVANOVIC Chargé d'affaires a.i.
