Peacebuilding Commission Fifth session Organizational Committee Letter dated 18 October 2011 from the Chair of the Peacebuilding Commission addressed to the Permanent Representative of Australia to the United Nations I have the honour to refer to your letters dated 19 July 2011 expressing the interest of Australia in participating in the Burundi and Liberia configurations of the Peacebuilding Commission. I am pleased to inform you that the requests contained in your aforementioned letters have met with no objection from the members of the Organizational Committee of the Peacebuilding Commission. Therefore, Australia is invited to participate in all future meetings of the Burundi and Liberia configurations of the Commission, in accordance with paragraph 7 (b) of General Assembly resolution 60/180 and Security Council resolution 1645 (2005).
