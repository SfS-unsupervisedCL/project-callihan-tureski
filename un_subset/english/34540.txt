%%%POLITICAL CONDITIONS|SUDAN|

Official communiqué of the 5322nd (closed) meeting of the Security Council Held in private at Headquarters, New York, on Tuesday, 13 December 2005, at 10.45 a.m. In accordance with rule 55 of the provisional rules of procedure of the Security Council, the following communiqué was issued through the Secretary-General in place of a verbatim record: "At its 5322nd meeting, held in private on 13 December 2005, the Security Council considered the item entitled `Reports of the Secretary-General on the Sudan'. "In accordance with the decision taken at the 5321st meeting, the President extended an invitation under rule 39 of the Council's provisional rules of procedure to Mr. Luis Moreno-Ocampo, Prosecutor of the International Criminal Court. "The members of the Council and Mr. Luis Moreno-Ocampo, Prosecutor of the International Criminal Court, had an exchange of views after the briefing."
