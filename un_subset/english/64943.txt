Peacebuilding Commission Sixth session Organizational Committee Letter dated 27 March 2012 from the Chair of the Peacebuilding Commission addressed to the Permanent Representative of Turkey to the United Nations I have the honour to refer to your letter dated 20 March 2012 expressing the interest of Turkey in participating in the Sierra Leone configuration of the Peacebuilding Commission. I am pleased to inform you that the request contained in your aforementioned letter has met with no objection from the members of the Organizational Committee of the Peacebuilding Commission. Therefore, Turkey is invited to participate in all meetings of the Sierra Leone configuration of the Commission, in accordance with paragraph 7 (b) of General Assembly resolution 60/180 and Security Council resolution 1645 (2005). (Signed) Abulkalam Abdul Momen Chair
