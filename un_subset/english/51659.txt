Letter dated 13 January 2009 from the Permanent Observer of Palestine to the United Nations addressed to the President of the Security Council I have the honour to request that, in accordance with its previous practice the Security Council invite the Permanent Observer of Palestine to the United Nations to participate in the meeting of the Security Council that will be held on Wednesday, 14 January 2009, on the protection of civilians in armed conflict. (Signed) Riyad Mansour Ambassador Permanent Observer
