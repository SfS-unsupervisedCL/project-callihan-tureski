99-03674 (E) 120299 United Nations A/53/440/Add.1 General Assembly Distr.: General 11 February 1999 Original: English Fifty-third session Agenda item 16 (a) Elections to fill vacancies in subsidiary organs and other elections: election of seven members of the Committee for Programme and Coordination Election of seven members of the Committee for Programme and Coordination Note by the Secretary-General Addendum 1. By its decision 1998/202 B of 7 May 1998, the Economic and Social Council, in addition to nominating Member States for election by the General Assembly at its fifty-third session to the Committee for Programme and Coordination (see A/53/440), postponed to a future session the nomination of one member from African States for a term beginning on the date of election and expiring on 31 December 2001 and one member from Western European and other States for a term beginning on the date of election and expiring on 31 December 1999. 2. At its organizational session for 1999, the Council nominated the Comoros as the member from the African States for election by the Assembly (see Council decision 1999/210).
