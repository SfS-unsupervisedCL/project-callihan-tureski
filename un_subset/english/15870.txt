Report by the Secretary-General concerning the credentials of the alternate representative of Ukraine on the Security Council Pursuant to rule 15 of the provisional rules of procedure of the Security Council, the Secretary-General wishes to report that he has received a note verbale dated 30 August 2001 from the Permanent Mission of Ukraine to the United Nations stating that Mr. Andriy Beshta has been appointed alternate representative of the Ukraine on the Security Council. In the opinion of the Secretary-General, that communication constitutes adequate provisional credentials.
