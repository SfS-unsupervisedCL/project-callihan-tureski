Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1298 (2000) concerning the situation between Eritrea and Ethiopia Note verbale dated 14 June 2000 from the Permanent Mission of the Republic of the Sudan to the United Nations addressed to the Secretary-General The Permanent Representative of the Sudan to the United Nations presents his compliments to the Secretary-General of the United Nations and, with reference to his note No. SCA/2/00(7) dated 1 June 2000 concerning Security Council resolution 1298 (2000), adopted by the Council on 17 May 2000, has the honour to indicate that the competent Sudanese authorities have taken the necessary steps to implement the provisions set out in paragraph (6) (a) and (b) of resolution 1298 (2000).
