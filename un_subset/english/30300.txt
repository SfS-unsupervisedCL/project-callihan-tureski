Fifty-ninth session Request for the inclusion of an additional item in the agenda of the fifty-ninth session Financing of the United Nations Mission in the Sudan Note by the Secretary-General 1. The Secretary-General has the honour to request, pursuant to rule 15 of the rules of procedure of the General Assembly, the inclusion in the agenda of the fifty-ninth session of the General Assembly of an additional item entitled "Financing of the United Nations Mission in the Sudan". 2. Owing to the nature of the item, the Secretary-General further requests that it be allocated to the Fifth Committee.
